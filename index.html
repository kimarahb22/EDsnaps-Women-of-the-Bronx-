Law & Politics

Arly Phoenix

💡 Pelham Pkwy (5 Train)

Born: June 25, 1954

Hometown: South Bronx, NY

Historical Impact

Appointed in 2009, she made history as the first Latina and third woman Supreme Court Justice. She grew up in the Bronxdale Houses right near the Morrison Av station, walking these exact neighborhood streets during her youth.

Researched By:

Name: Laimaur, Age 13

Why I chose her: "I think Justice Sotomayor is incredibly impactful because she proved that someone from a humble background in the Bronx could reach the highest court in America. She inspires me to pursue my dreams in law."

Arts & Entertainment

Jennifer Lopez

🎵 Pelham Pkwy (2 Train)

Born: July 24, 1969

Hometown: Castle Hill, Bronx

Historical Impact

A global pop icon, actress, and dancer who famously put her Bronx roots on the map with her smash hit debut album and title track 'On the 6'. The project was named directly after her daily transit commute line into Manhattan.

Researched By:

Name: Kyleigh, Age 14

Why I chose her: "I love music and dance, and J.Lo shows how powerful Bronx creativity is on a global scale. She never forgot where she came from, which makes me proud of my borough."
