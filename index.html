Law & Politics

Sonia Sotomayor

💡 Morrison Av-Soundview (6 Train)

Born: June 25, 1954

Hometown: South Bronx, NY

Historical Impact

Appointed in 2009, she made history as the first Latina and third woman Supreme Court Justice. She grew up in the Bronxdale Houses right near the Morrison Av station, walking these exact neighborhood streets during her youth.

Arts & Entertainment

Jennifer Lopez

🎵 Castle Hill Av (6 Train)

Born: July 24, 1969

Hometown: Castle Hill, Bronx

Historical Impact

A global pop icon, actress, and dancer who famously put her Bronx roots on the map with her smash hit debut album and title track 'On the 6'. The project was named directly after her daily transit commute line into Manhattan.
